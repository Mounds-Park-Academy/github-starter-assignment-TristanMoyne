Nuclear Power!

learn more about the radioactive world of nuclear power!

Nuclear Power is crucial for our world, from the power plants to the sun so it's crucial to how we live. Nuclear power is easily divided into 2 different reactions, fusion and fission. Fission is the nuclear reaction we use the most today, it happens when a neutron hits a heavy atom like uranium 235 (which is the most commonly used atom). The atom spilts and causes a chain reaction splitting more atoms which leads to the energy being transferred into heat, heating up water, releasing steam, moving a turbine thus creating electricity. The other way this energy can be transferred is in nuclear weapons, where the fission reaction will create a large ball of fire, vaporizing everything in it's vicinity and releasing a lot of radioactive particles.

  • Nuclear Power has 2 different reaction types
  • Nuclear power is used to produce eelectricity and to create bombs
